What is Riot Games?

Riot Games is video game development company famous for many titles such as League of Legends, Valorant, Legends of Runeterra, Teamfight Tactics, and more recently, League of Legends: Wild Rift.


What is Esports?

To make the long story short, Esports is basically traditional sports, but its Electronic, hence E-sports.
Like traditional sports, there are different sports like ball games, but for Esports, it's the video game genre.


What Esports does Riot cater to?

Riot's Esports catalog consists of the 5 games they have currently.

  • League of Legends
  • Valorant
  • League of Legends: Wild Rift
  • Teamfight Tactics
  • Legends of Runeterra
